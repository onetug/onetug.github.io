# Orlando .NET User Group (ONETUG)

## About Us

ONETUG is a community based in Central Florida. Our roots are based in Microsoft's .NET ecosystem. We hold monthly meetups and social gatherings for community members.

[Sign up for our Meetups](https://www.meetup.com/onetug/events/)

[Follow us on Twitter](https://twitter.com/onetug)

[Follow us on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/1550507)

[Like and Subscribe on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC-9-kyTW8ZkZNDHQJ6Fgpwg)

[Join our Facebook Group](https://www.facebook.com/groups/onetug/)
